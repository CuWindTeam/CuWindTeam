CU Wind Team

The CU wind team is a mechanical engineering team that competes in the NREL collegite wind competition(CWC). We strive to make the most effective turbine possible within the rules of the competition. Each year we start by designing and building a new turbine with the lessons learned from previous years. Other aspects of the competition involve comunity work and informational interview along with the design and evaluation of a wind farm at a selected location. With the opertunity to compete our team will go to the annual competition at a designated NREL location.
